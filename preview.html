The problem

Your customers are already telling you what to build. You just don't have time to listen.

500tickets/week

The signal is there. The time isn't.

Every complaint, every rage quit, every feature request is already in your Zendesk and Intercom. But nobody reads all 500. Patterns that would actually move retention never make it into the planning doc.

2 wkswait on the analyst

The dashboard you asked for ships after the decision.

Product Managers file a data request, the analyst writes SQL, the chart lands a week later. By then you've already shipped on vibes. The rest of the org builds its own private dashboards, and nobody agrees on the numbers.

3hires per team

Analysts, researchers, product ops — all billing for glue work.

Orgs staff up entire functions just to turn raw signal into decisions. Most of that work is synthesis the LLM era should have collapsed by now. Budget-constrained teams just… skip it.

One chat. Your whole product stack. Answers grounded in real signal.

How it works

From zero to your first insight in under ten minutes.
